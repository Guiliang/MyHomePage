Research Interests

Embodied Decision-Making (Our lab intro). My research focuses on the intersection of embodied intelligence and decision-making systems, particularly those driven by reinforcement learning algorithms. I am particularly interested in investigating decision-making in safety-critical scenarios, with the goal of developing robust and reliable control methods for various embodied agents. The decision-making process can be informed by diverse signals, such as expert demonstrations, human feedback, or social norms.
  • Whole-body Control for Humanoid Robots;
  • Generalist Robotic Manipulation System (a.k.a Vision-Language-Action, VLA);
  • Generative Skill Acquisition from Simulation;
  • Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF);
For more details, please visit our Embodied Decision-Making (EdeM) Lab.
