About Me

I am an PhD Candidate at School of Computing Science at Simon Fraser University. Supervised by Professor Oliver Schulte , I have spending three year working on the Deep Reinforcement Learning (DRL) and it's novel application in the Sport Analytic . We have built a DRL-based player ranking system and developed a novel metric to estimated the value of players, using the player-by-play Video Tracking dataset provided by sportlogiq.

I received my Bachelor degree at South China University of Technology, major in Computing Science.
