Research Interests

Reinforcement Learning (RL) and the related topics, including, Inverse Constrained RL, Model-based RL, Interpretable RL, Agent Evaluation and Embedding. I have also studied applications on Natural Language Process (NLP), Sports analytic and Autonomous Driving.
