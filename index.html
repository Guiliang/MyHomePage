About Me

I am currently working as a postdoctoral fellow at University of Waterloo and Vector Institute with Prof. Pascal Poupart.

Graduate School: I received my Ph.D. degree at the School of Computing Science, Simon Fraser University under the supervision of Prof. Oliver Schulte. To explore my research interest in a variety of environments, I have finished two research internships at Cognitive Computing Lab, Baidu Research (supervised by Prof. Ping Li) and SLiQ Lab, Sportlogiq.

Undergraduate School: I received my Bachelor degree at the School of Computing Science and Engineering, South China University of Technology.
